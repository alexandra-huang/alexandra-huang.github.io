About Us

MatchDog is a nonprofit organization that aims to help the right dogs find the right homes  and families. Through our cutting edge artificial intelligence algorithm, we make finding the right dog for you easy. Simply take a short quiz in order to get matched up with your perfect dog. Or if you'd like to find a dog the old-fashioned way, you can browse through a list of all of our lovely dogs.
