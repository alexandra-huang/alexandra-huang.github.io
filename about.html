About me

I'm a product designer and second-year Master's student in the Human Factors and Ergonomics program at San Jose State University. I'm particularly interested in designing experiences and products that are intuitive and easy-to-use. I believe in improving quality of life through beautiful, enjoyable, and seamless designs.

Random Facts:


  • I did dragon boat for all four years of undergrad.
  • I've done the Turkey Trot for the last eight years (and counting!).
  • I got into design/HCI after taking my first HCI class in undergrad and feeling like everything I was learning about in class should be common sense. I was surprised to find that it wasn't, so here we are, working to improve designs and apply my knowledge of how people think, behave, and act to make the world a safer, more user-friendly place.

Education:


  • M.S. in Human Factors and Ergonomics - San Jose State University (expected graduation May 2020)
  • B.S. in Cognitive Science with Specialization in Human Computer Interaction - University of California, San Diego
